Upcoming retreat

Sattva Path Retreat

A two-day retreat for adults seeking peace, acceptance, spiritual guidance, and simple meditation practices that can be carried into daily life.

Registration is closed for this retreat. You are welcome to reach out with questions or hear about the next one.

DatesSeptember 19-20, 2026
LocationEnchanted Hills Retreat, 3568 Mt Veeder Rd, Napa, CA 94558
OrganizerSattva Path Collective LLC
Age18 years or older
  • Feel more peaceful and grounded in your current season of life.
  • Learn simple meditation practices for everyday living.
  • Release pressure around what has passed or what feels lost.
  • Trust that your path can unfold without force.
  • Return to daily life with more acceptance, clarity, and steadiness.
  • Remember that you are in the right place at the right time.
