About the path

Meet the host: a gentle story, not a performance.

Dr Nirupama Gupta's path into spiritual guidance began quietly, through her own search for light during a difficult season. She knows how it can feel to move through uncertainty, questions, and heaviness while still showing up for everyday life.

Over time, meditation, reflection, and gentle spiritual practice helped her return to peace, acceptance, and trust in the present moment. Sattva Path Collective grew from that lived experience: a wish to remind others that healing does not require abandoning life, forcing perfection, or becoming someone else. It can begin exactly where a person is.

Before this offering took form, she walked through many paths of study, discipline, and service. Her early work led her deeply into science, where she earned a PhD in Biochemistry from the University of Michigan Medical School. Her professional life later moved into the IT field, where she continues to work with structure, problem-solving, and project leadership, supported by her PMP certification. Alongside this, her study of Hindustani classical vocal music and her journey toward Sangeet Visharad continue to keep her connected to devotion, practice, and inner listening.

Along the way, she has also learned from respected teachers and traditions that deepened her understanding of inner healing. Her studies include yoga, breathwork, meditation, Reiki, Vaastu, hypnosis, age regression, and past-life regression, including a workshop personally taught by Dr. Brian Weiss. She continues to see herself as a lifelong student, approaching each learning experience with humility, curiosity, and reverence.

Over time, these different paths began to meet in a quieter place. Science taught her to observe with care, technology taught her to work with complexity, music taught her surrender and devotion, and meditation taught her to return to the stillness beneath it all. For her, spirituality is not about leaving the world behind. It is about learning how to live within it with presence, acceptance, and trust.

Her guidance is gentle, grounded, and respectful of each person's own path. She offers meditation, reflection, and spiritual support for those seeking clarity, peace, and a deeper connection with themselves. She does not see healing as something one person gives to another. Instead, she hopes to create a safe and sincere space where each individual can reconnect with the wisdom, resilience, and quiet peace already within them. The path belongs to each person; she simply walks alongside for a while.
